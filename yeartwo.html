Homecoming! Part 2

We went to homecoming again! I just want to say dancing with you is one of my favorite things in the world. I am NOT a good dancer at all but holding you close and swaying to the music makes me forget about that.

Even more dates

We went on a buttload more dates this last year! Unfortunately we couldn't go on as many as I would've liked due to stinky circumstances but we still had fun at Shaw Farm, USS Nightmare, the cat cafe, Outback, and an amazing photoshoot to attempt to cheer you up.

Two years!

Here is where we are now! It's been two whole years! Well, with a couple weeks taken out somewhere in the middle I guess, but I realize now more than ever that you're the one I want to be with. You bring me so much joy every time I see you or talk to you. Even if one of us is being lame or moody, we get over it quick and can find ourselves laughing again in no time. I'm so glad to have been with you these last two years and know that we have many more to come. I love you to the moon and back!
