Homecoming!

This was our first dance ever! We ate at Panera and took pictures with your friends at the park. I remember being a little scared of seeing your whole family. But it was a great and memorable night!

Many, many dates

We went on a boatload of dates this first year! Movies, bowling, Halloween, plenty of starbucks and fast food, and lots of hugs and kisses.

Prom

After a wild ride we were actually able to go to prom! We had a good time at Paul Brown Stadium and you took care of me while I was feeling really bad. I hope we're able to go to one more so I can make it up to you.

One Year Down

Our anniversary took us to a fancy restaurant and a movie. Our first year went pretty quickly to me. We had made fun of other couples for talking about their future so early in the relationship, but I came to find out I'd want to be with you forever in this first 365 days! You were already the love of my life!
